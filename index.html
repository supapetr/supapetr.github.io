Story

I am currently a research intern at Medical Psychology department at Amsterdam UMC. Pursuing a double Research Master's at Radboud University in Cognitive Neuroscience and Behavioural Science. My journey started at Charles University in Prague, blending social sciences and humanities. Today, I am focused on "real-world problem" oriented research, prioritizing a people-centred approach, diversity, equity, and inclusion.
